4-step building process

Build the
connection.

The Voss-based four-step process for turning a surface conversation into a genuine human connection. NeverSettle scores and tracks every step.

1

Pick the word

Find an emotion word or phrase the candidate used. Mirror it back. Ask what it means to them.

2

Ask why it matters

Once you understand the definition — ask why. If they got a million dollars tomorrow, why in the world would this still matter?

3

Challenge them

They have made a statement about who they are. Make them validate it with a concrete example. Real or perceived — challenge it.

4

Complement and ingroup

Complement them on who they want to be. Create an ingroup feeling. That's why we're the winners — we do things differently.
